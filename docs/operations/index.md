## How to Configure

### General Settings

Go to **Settings** (Gear Icon).

- **Theme:** Switch between Light and Dark mode.
- **Language:** Change the app language.
- **Notifications:** Turn alerts on or off.

### Advanced Settings

*For advanced users only.*

- **Database Path:** Change where data is stored.
- **API Keys:** Connect to third-party services.

## FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

**Q: Where is my data saved?**
A: By default, it's in your Documents folder.

**Q: Can I use this offline?**
A: Yes! You don't need internet for basic features.

**Q: How do I update?**
A: Download the new version from our website and install it.
