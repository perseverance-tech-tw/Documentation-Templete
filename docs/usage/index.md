# Usage Guide

**Goal:** Teach users how to use the application day-to-day.

---

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Open the App

- **Windows:** Double-click the icon on your desktop.
- **Mac:** Open from Applications.
- **Linux:** Run from terminal.

### Step 2: First Setup

1. **Create an Account** (if needed).
2. **Adjust Settings** like language or theme.
3. **Connect** your data source.

---

## Common Tasks

### How to Create a New Item

1. Click the **New** button (+).
2. Fill in the details.
3. Click **Save**.

### How to Edit

1. Click on the item you want to change.
2. Make your edits.
3. Click **Save**.

### How to Export Data

1. Go to **File > Export**.
2. Choose a format (PDF, Excel, etc.).
3. Save it to your computer.

---

## Tips and Tricks

!!! tip "Did you know?"
    You can use keyboard shortcuts like `Ctrl+S` to save quickly!

!!! warning "Backup"
    Remember to export your data once in a while to keep it safe.
